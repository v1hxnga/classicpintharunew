FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Welcome to our FAQ page, where we aim to address all your queries and provide comprehensive information about our products and services. Here, you'll find answers to common questions regarding sizing, customization options, delivery times, and more. We understand that choosing the perfect attire for your special occasion can be overwhelming, which is why we're dedicated to offering clear and transparent guidance every step of the way. Whether you're inquiring about our bridal collections, groom wear, event management services, or occasional wear, we're here to assist you in making informed decisions and ensuring your experience with us is seamless and enjoyable.

MOST FREQUENT QUESTION

What are your business hours and days of operation?

Our business hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, every day of the week. We're here to assist you throughout the week for all your bridal and groom wear needs.

Are you open on weekends or public holidays?

We are open on weekends, but please note that we are closed on public holidays and Poya days. However, you can still reach out to us via email or our website, and we'll be happy to assist you with any inquiries or appointments.

Do you offer alterations services for bridal/groom attire?

Absolutely! We offer a comprehensive range of alteration services to ensure your bridal and groom attire fits you perfectly. From minor adjustments to major modifications, our skilled team is here to tailor your garments to your exact specifications for a flawless look on your special day.

How can I contact your customer support team for further assistance?

You can reach our customer support team for further assistance through multiple channels. Feel free to call us at 0112 667 536, message us on WhatsApp at 0778597319, or send us an email at pintharu117@gmail.com We're here to help!

Do you accept walk-in appointments, or should I schedule in advance?

Absolutely! While we do accept walk-in appointments, we recommend scheduling in advance for a more personalized experience. By making an appointment, we can ensure dedicated time to discuss your needs and preferences thoroughly. Feel free to reach out to us to schedule your appointment, or simply drop by during our business hours for assistance!

Can I order custom-designed bridal/groom attire?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom-designed bridal and groom attire tailored to your unique preferences. We invite you to visit our office, where our team will work closely with you to bring your vision to life and create the perfect ensemble for your special day.

What types of events do you specialize in managing?

We specialize in managing a wide range of events, including comprehensive wedding management services encompassing every aspect of your special day. Additionally, we excel in event decoration and coordination, ensuring your venue is transformed into a captivating space. Furthermore, we offer expertise in handling the entire homecoming celebration, ensuring a seamless and memorable experience for you and your loved ones.
